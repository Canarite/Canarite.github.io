Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ariteWorks, an independent mobile game developer, handles information when you use games developed and published by ariteWorks.

1. Information Collection and Use

AriteWorks games are designed to be enjoyed without user registration. AriteWorks does not collect or store personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers on its own servers.

2. Automatically Collected Information and Third-Party Services

To operate games and display advertisements, ariteWorks games may use third-party services. These services may collect non-personal information used to identify your device.

Third-party services used may include:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Unity Ads (video and interstitial advertising)

These services may collect information such as IP address, device information, and advertising identifiers (IDFA / AAID) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Permissions

AriteWorks games may require internet access to load game content and display advertisements. No unnecessary device permissions are requested.

4. Children’s Privacy

ariteWorks does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that such information has been collected, please contact AriteWorks and it will be removed.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and are effective immediately.
