Privacy Policy

AriteWorks is an independent mobile game developer. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how information is handled in AriteWorks games.

Information Collection

AriteWorks games do not collect personal information such as name, address, or email.

Advertising

Games may display ads provided by third-party services such as Google AdMob and Unity Ads. These services may collect non-personal data, such as device information or advertising identifiers, to show relevant ads.

Analytics

Basic anonymous analytics may be used to understand how players interact with the games and to improve gameplay experience.

Children’s Information

AriteWorks games do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that such data has been collected, please contact me and it will be removed.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
